Our service runs on a model, and boy is it a fun one.

Our model is designed to not bore you. We made it from the ground up, scouring the web for games and designing modules to be comprehensive and fun.

In every 'module' that you do, we throw a few reading bits here and there, but we are constantly changing...

Also, as a bonus, our modules aren't completed on paper. All modules are completed using third-party services to construct results and deliver them to you. This is still a work in progress though.

And thats the thing... it runs in your browser!

We have designed LUDO to work natively in your web browser to bring you the full experience.

Below is a list of supported web browsers or browsers we recommend:

  • Firefox version 72 or higher
  • Opera Browser (except versions that dont support SSL 3.0)
  • Microsoft Edge (Beta, Canary and Nightly is also supported!)
  • Google Chrome version 72 or higher

Below is a list of browsers we don't support:

  • Internet Explorer 11 or lower
